How We Work

A Methodology Built on Verification

Every ISSLA Risk engagement follows a structured three-phase intelligence cycle — ensuring that every conclusion in your report can withstand professional scrutiny.

01

OSINT — Open Source Intelligence

Intelligence Layer 01

We begin with a structured open-source sweep: corporate registries, court records, regulatory filings, sanctions databases, adverse media archives, and official government sources. This phase builds the foundational data layer for every engagement and establishes a baseline timeline of the subject's activities.

Corporate Registries Sanctions Screening Court Records Adverse Media
02

HUMINT — Human Intelligence

Intelligence Layer 02

Where documents end, people begin. Our regional source networks provide ground-level verification that no database can replicate: confirmation of business activity, reputational standing among peers, behavioral patterns, and relationships that formal records never capture. All HUMINT is handled under strict operational security protocols.

Source Networks Discreet Inquiries Reputational Verification Operational Security
03

SOCMIN — Social Media Intelligence

Intelligence Layer 03

Digital footprints reveal what formal sources conceal. ISSLA Risk analysts conduct structured analysis of professional and social media profiles, public statements, network affiliations, and online behavioral patterns. This layer is particularly valuable for identifying inconsistencies between stated positions and observed conduct.

Profile Analysis Network Mapping Behavioral Patterns Digital Footprint

Core Capabilities

What Sets ISSLA Risk Apart

01

Regional Depth

Specialist expertise in Latin American and Caribbean markets — jurisdictions where informal networks, political dynamics, and opacity of records demand analysts with genuine on-the-ground knowledge, not just database access.

02

Multi-Layer Verification

No finding reaches your report without independent corroboration. Every significant claim is verified across at least two independent sources — eliminating the rumor-as-fact problem that plagues lesser intelligence products.

03

Legal & Compliance Integration

Our analysts understand how reports will be used — in investment committee presentations, legal proceedings, regulatory filings, and board-level decisions. Reports are structured to meet the evidentiary standards of your specific use case.

04

Absolute Discretion

Every engagement is confidential by default. We do not confirm or deny client relationships, we do not use client information for any purpose beyond the specific engagement, and all deliverables are transmitted through secure channels.
