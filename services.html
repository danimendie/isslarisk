Service 01

Global Due Diligence

Global Due Diligence is our most comprehensive service, designed for M&A transactions, joint ventures, high-value counterparty relationships, and critical hiring decisions. We go beyond database searches to surface the information that defines whether a deal, partnership, or appointment carries hidden risk.

Our due diligence engagements cover corporate structure and beneficial ownership, financial history and litigation exposure, regulatory sanctions and AML/KYC compliance, corporate property mapping, and UBO identification — delivered in tiered report formats calibrated to your risk exposure and decision timeline.

  • — Litigation history and legal exposure

  • — Financial background analysis
  • — UBO identification
  • — Multi-jurisdictional coverage

Service 02

Background Analysis

Background Analysis is a focused intelligence engagement designed for specific individuals or entities where a full due diligence scope is not warranted. It is the preferred service for executive vetting, board-level appointments, key hire evaluations, and preliminary pre-engagement screening.

We apply open-source intelligence methodology to build a verified profile of the subject — drawing on corporate registries, court records, media archives, professional networks, and sector-specific databases — and identify flags that conventional HR or legal review would not surface.

What's Included

  • — Corporate registry and directorship mapping
  • — Court records and litigation history
  • — Media archive review and adverse flag identification
  • — Professional network and association analysis
  • — Sector-specific database screening
  • — Summary risk assessment with confidence ratings

Best For

Executive Vetting Board Appointments Pre-Hire Screening Partnership Evaluation Investment Pre-Qualification

Delivery 5–10 business days
Languages English and Spanish
Scope Individual or entity
Available as standalone or as pre-qualification for full due diligence

Service 03

Reputation Analysis

Reputation Analysis is an intelligence product focused on the public and non-public narrative surrounding a person, entity, or brand. It is designed for clients who need to understand how a subject is perceived — across media, professional networks, sector channels, and open-source environments — before entering a relationship with them.

We identify reputation risk vectors: adverse media patterns, controversy associations, network-level risks, and discrepancies between a subject's stated and observed profile. The deliverable is a calibrated risk assessment, not a media summary.

What's Included

  • — Adverse media pattern identification
  • — Controversy and association risk mapping
  • — Stated vs. observed profile discrepancy analysis
  • — Network-level exposure assessment
  • — Digital and social media footprint review
  • — Calibrated risk rating with sourced findings
What It Surfaces
Adverse Media Pattern-based, not keyword-based
Controversy Historical and emerging associations
Discrepancies Stated vs. observed profile gaps
Network Risk Indirect exposures via associations
Digital Footprint Online presence and signal mapping
Deliverable: calibrated risk assessment with source documentation

Best For

Pre-Partnership Review Brand Association Risk Investor Relations Public-Facing Appointments

Service 04

Country Risk & Political Forecast

Our Country Risk assessments deliver a structured analysis of the political, regulatory, security, and operational environment in a target jurisdiction. They are designed for organizations planning market entry, asset protection strategies, or operational continuity planning in complex or high-risk environments.

Coverage is deepest in Latin America, the Caribbean, and the Middle East — markets where political volatility, regulatory opacity, and ESG factors combine to create risks that standard market research does not capture.

What's Included

  • — Political scenario analysis and forecasting
  • — Regulatory and compliance overview
  • — ESG risk factors and exposure mapping
  • — Security environment assessment
  • — Operational recommendations
