Service 03

Reputational Intelligence

Reputational Intelligence is an intelligence product focused on the public and non-public narrative surrounding a person, entity, or brand. It is designed for clients who need to understand how a subject is perceived — across media, professional networks, and open-source environments — before committing capital or entering a relationship.

We identify reputational risk vectors: adverse media patterns, controversy associations, network-level risks, and discrepancies between a subject's stated and observed profile. The deliverable is actionable intelligence built to withstand scrutiny — not a media summary.

What's Included

  • — Adverse media pattern identification
  • — Controversy and association risk mapping
  • — Stated vs. observed profile discrepancy analysis
  • — Network-level exposure assessment
  • — Digital and social media footprint review
  • — Calibrated risk rating with sourced findings
What It Surfaces
Adverse Media Pattern-based, not keyword-based
Controversy Historical and emerging associations
Discrepancies Stated vs. observed profile gaps
Network Risk Indirect exposures via associations
Digital Footprint Online presence and signal mapping
Deliverable: calibrated risk assessment with source documentation

Best For

Pre-Partnership Review Brand Association Risk Investor Relations Public-Facing Appointments

Service 04

Jurisdictional Risk Assessment

Our Jurisdictional Risk Assessments deliver structured analysis of the political, regulatory, security, and operational environment in a target jurisdiction. Designed for organizations planning market entry, capital deployment, or operational continuity planning in high-risk and opaque jurisdictions.

Coverage is deepest in Latin America, Central America, the Caribbean, and emerging markets — jurisdictions where political volatility, regulatory opacity, and political exposure combine to create risks that standard market research does not capture.

What's Included

  • — Risk rating framework by domain
  • — Political scenario analysis and forecasting
  • — Regulatory and compliance overview
  • — ESG risk factors and exposure mapping
  • — Security environment assessment
  • — Operational recommendations
